Authorcraft

Description

Authorcraft is the only literature club of NMAMIT that mainly focuses on showcasing the talent of students in creative writing,long form writings, poetry, shayaris, and standup comedies in all languages and all forms.

The club's main motive is to help people improve their literary skills and promote these talents on public platforms.

Multiple general meets, open mic, and steal the spotlight (the core event ) is conducted every year to gather like-minded students that wish to focus on literary skills and give them a platform to showcase the same
