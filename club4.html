Annadana Club

Description

We are an NGO dedicated to solve and spread awareness related to hunger and starvation. We regularly donate groceries to Orphanages, old age homes and special schools. We dance sing and play with them. This year we have a conducted rice donation drive as well. Anna means rice and daana means donation so it justifies what we do. We also organised a drama last year which depicted the difficulties of old people who are sent off to old age homes. This is to spread awareness amongst the youth.
