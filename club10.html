Counselling & Outreach for Peer Empowerment(COPE) is a student-led mental health club at NMAMIT is promoting mental well-being among youngsters and the college students. Comprised of empathetic and proactive students trained by a professional from Mpower, this group recognizes the unique stressors faced in a teens life without the barriers of judgment . we have successfully organized events and plan on coming up with more soon enough to have a healthy mental state for youths and students . These activities aim to destigmatize mental health issues, encourage open conversations, and provide practical coping strategies among youngsters to help them lead in a stress free environment.
